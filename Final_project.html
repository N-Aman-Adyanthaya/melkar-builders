Who We Are

The Melkar Story

Melkar Builders was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves a home built with care, quality, and honesty. Founded in Melkar, Panemangalore, we set out to change the way people experience home buying and construction in the coastal Karnataka region.

"A home is the greatest investment of your life. We treat it that way."

Over the years, we have delivered residential apartments and commercial spaces that stand as testaments to our commitment. Every brick we lay, every beam we raise, carries our promise of excellence.

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of the most trusted builders in Panemangalore, our journey has been defined by satisfied families and landmarks that dot the skyline of Melkar.
